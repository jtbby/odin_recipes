The Best Eggs!

The easiest and tastiest scrambled egg recipe!

A simple recipe that your scrambled eggs to the next level, perfect for egg lovers!

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of unsalted butter
  • A teaspoon of salt
  • A tablespoon of heavy cream
  • (Optional) a teaspoon of parsley

Steps

  1. Heat a pan to medium-high
  2. Whisk together the eggs, heavy cream, and salt
  3. Once the pan is heated, place the butter in and wait until it is golden brown
  4. Pour the egg mixture on the pan and whisk thoroughly to your desired doneness
  5. remove from the pan and granish with parsley
