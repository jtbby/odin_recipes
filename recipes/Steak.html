The perfect steak

A delicious and easy steak recipe!

A straight forward steak recipe to ensure your steaks always come out perfect

Ingredients

  • 8-16oz Steak(Ribeye, New York Strip, Sirloin)
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of pepper
  • 2 strands of rosemary
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • One tablespoon of avocado oil
  • (Optional but preferred) 1 cast iron skillet

Steps

  1. Remove steak from the fridge and allow it to sit in room temp for 30 minutes
  2. Pour the oil in the skillet and pre heat it to medium-high
  3. Pat dry the steak, and season with salt and pepper
  4. Place the steak in the skillet, and sear both sides for 3-4 minutes
  5. lower the heat to medium low and add butter, rosemary, and garlic
  6. baste the steak with butter and cook to desired doneness
  7. place the steak on a cutting board and slice against the grain, and enjoy!
